Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and process

What types of projects do you specialize in?

I specialize in backend development for web applications, particularly those requiring robust, scalable architectures. This includes e-commerce platforms, financial systems, content management systems, and data-intensive applications. My expertise is in Java and Spring Boot, but I also work with other technologies as needed.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple API might take 2-4 weeks, while a comprehensive microservices architecture could take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements. I always aim to be transparent about timelines and keep you updated on progress throughout the development process.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages for all projects I develop. Each service package includes a specific support period, and I also provide extended maintenance contracts for clients who need long-term support. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and minor feature enhancements.

How do you handle project communication?

Clear communication is essential for project success. I typically use a combination of regular video calls, email updates, and project management tools like Jira or Trello. I provide weekly progress reports and am available for questions via email or chat during business hours. For urgent matters, I'm also available by phone.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and the remaining 50% upon project completion for smaller projects. For larger projects, I offer milestone-based payments (e.g., 30% upfront, 30% at midpoint, 40% upon completion). I can also accommodate monthly retainer arrangements for ongoing development work. All payment terms are clearly outlined in our contract.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes, I work with clients globally. I'm comfortable with remote collaboration and have experience working across different time zones. I use tools like Zoom, Slack, and project management software to ensure smooth communication regardless of location. I'm also flexible with scheduling meetings to accommodate different time zones.
